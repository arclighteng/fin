How to download from your bank
  1. Sign in at chase.com
  2. Go to your checking or credit card account
  3. Click Download account activity (top right)
  4. Choose CSV as the file type and select your date range
  5. Click Download and upload the file above
  1. Sign in at bankofamerica.com
  2. Select your account and click Download
  3. Choose Microsoft Excel format (OFX) — or CSV if available
  4. Select date range and click Download transactions
  1. Sign in at americanexpress.com
  2. Go to Statements & Activity
  3. Click Download and choose CSV
  4. Pick your date range and download the file
  1. Sign in at wellsfargo.com
  2. Select your account and go to Account Activity
  3. Click Download Account Activity
  4. Choose Comma-Separated Values (CSV) and your date range
  5. Click Download
  1. Sign in at capitalone.com
  2. Select your account
  3. Click Download transactions
  4. Choose CSV and your date range
  5. Click Download
